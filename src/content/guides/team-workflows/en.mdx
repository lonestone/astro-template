## Weekly planning

Keep it short (30 minutes). One person presents priorities, everyone comments. Decisions go into the shared log.

## Bi-weekly retrospective

Three columns: **what went well**, **what didn't**, **what we try next**. One hour, strict time-box.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tip">
  Save the retrospective as a template so you don't have to recreate it every fortnight.
</Callout>

## Monthly 1-on-1s

Same cadence for everyone. One page notes, three questions:

1. What's going well?
2. What's not?
3. What do you need from me?

The key is consistency, not length.
