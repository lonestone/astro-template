## Before you start

Make sure you have:

- An account (see [Getting started](/en/docs/getting-started))
- At least one teammate added to your project

## Step 1: Create the meeting

1. Open the **Meetings** section.
2. Click **Create**.
3. Fill in title, date, duration, and participants.

## Step 2: Build the agenda

Add one item per topic you want to discuss. Each item has a target duration so you can stay on time.

<Callout type="tip" title="Shortcut">
  Use a meeting template to pre-fill recurring agendas in one click.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Run the meeting

During the meeting:

- Follow the agenda in order.
- Capture decisions and next steps as you go.
- Assign owners for each action item.

## Step 4: Wrap up

Close the meeting. Your notes, decisions and next steps are saved automatically and available to every participant.
