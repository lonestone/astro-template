### North Wind helps companies navigate complex transformations.

For over a decade, North Wind has accompanied organizations through strategic pivots, mergers, and digital overhauls.

> **_Before, each launch felt like reinventing the wheel. Now we have a repeatable playbook that anyone on the team can pick up._**

Alex Rivera, Managing Partner

## The challenge

North Wind's launch checklists lived in a mix of Google Docs, Notion pages, and spreadsheets. Every new consultant had to piece together the process from scratch, and important steps occasionally slipped through.

The leadership team wanted one place where every step was visible, assigned, and dated, without adding another heavy tool.

## The solution

By switching to a structured, content-first workflow, North Wind now has:

- A single source of truth for every launch stage.
- Clear owner assignments that anyone can audit.
- Automated reminders that keep teams honest about deadlines.

The team reports a 45% reduction in average launch time and a sharp drop in last-minute fire drills.

> **_The biggest win is that our new consultants are productive on day one. They open the playbook, pick their role, and get going._**

Priya Shah, Consultant
