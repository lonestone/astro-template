### Bright Labs designs and builds digital products for ambitious clients.

Founded as a five-person studio, Bright Labs now counts 80 people across three continents, and they still ship like a startup.

> **_We were scared that growth would kill our agility. Turns out, with the right tools, the opposite is true._**

Maya Okonkwo, Co-founder

## The challenge

As the team grew, the founders noticed the usual warning signs: hallway decisions, duplicated work, long onboarding ramps, and a feeling that only a handful of people really knew what was going on.

They wanted to preserve their fast, transparent culture without turning into a mini-bureaucracy.

## The solution

Bright Labs adopted a lightweight governance model where:

- Every project has a public playbook that anyone can read and critique.
- Decisions are recorded in a shared log, searchable by role or by date.
- New joiners walk through the same onboarding path as everyone before them.

The outcome: a 60% shorter ramp-up time for new hires and a measurable uptick in cross-team collaboration.

> **_The magic isn't in one feature, it's in the fact that everyone has the same map. Decisions are out in the open, and nobody has to chase information._**

Tomás Varga, Operations Lead
