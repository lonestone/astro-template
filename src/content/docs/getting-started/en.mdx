## Create your account

1. Sign up at the URL you configured.
2. Confirm your email address.
3. Choose a display name.

You are now ready to create your first project.

## Create your first project

1. From the dashboard, click **Create project**.
2. Give it a name.
3. Pick a slug (used in URLs).

That's it. Your project is live.

<Callout type="info" title="Tip">
  You can rename the project later. The slug is fixed once created, so pick something short and stable.
</Callout>
