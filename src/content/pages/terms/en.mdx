# Terms of Sale

> This is a starter template. Replace it with terms reviewed by your legal counsel before going to production.

## 1. Purpose

These terms govern access to the platform operated by **Your Company Name** (the "Provider") and the services offered to users (the "Organization").

## 2. Access to the platform

The platform is accessible via the URLs provided in account creation. Access credentials are personal and confidential; the Organization is responsible for keeping them secret.

## 3. Plans and billing

Subscription plans are described on the [pricing page](/en/pricing). Billing is monthly or yearly depending on the plan chosen. Payment is due on the subscription date and renewed automatically.

## 4. Data ownership

The Organization remains the owner of all data transferred in connection with the use of the platform. The Provider processes this data solely to operate the service, under the terms of the [privacy policy](/en/privacy).

## 5. Liability

The Provider carries out its activity under an obligation of means. In the event of liability, the Provider's obligation shall be limited to the fees actually paid by the Organization.

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement in the event of a material breach, fifteen (15) days after a formal notice has been sent without remedy.

## 7. Applicable law

This agreement is governed by the law applicable in your jurisdiction.
