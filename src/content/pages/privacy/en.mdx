# Privacy Policy

> This is a starter template. Replace it with a policy reviewed by your legal counsel before going to production.

This privacy policy describes how **Your Company Name** (the "Company") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our website and services.

## 1. Data collected

To use our services, you provide personal data such as your name, email address and password. Without this minimum information, account creation is not possible.

When you use the platform, our servers automatically record session data, IP address, and information about your operating system and web browser.

### Cookies

The platform uses cookies to identify you upon login and to improve your experience. You can disable cookies by modifying your browser preferences; some features may then no longer work.

## 2. Retention

Data is retained for the entire duration of the service provision and for seven (7) days after account deletion.

Under applicable data protection law, you have a right of access, rectification, deletion and portability of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please write to us.

## 3. Use of data

Collected data is used to provide our services. The Company does not sell, lend or disclose your personal data to third parties, except:

- with your explicit consent;
- to comply with applicable laws or legal proceedings;
- to protect the rights, property or safety of the Company, its users or the public;
- to operate our services (payment providers, hosting providers, email providers), under strict contractual safeguards.

## 4. Security

Access to your account is protected by a password that you must keep confidential. The Company takes all reasonable measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, modification or destruction.

## 5. Changes

The Company may update this policy at any time. Significant changes will be announced on the website.

## 6. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact **hello@example.com**.
